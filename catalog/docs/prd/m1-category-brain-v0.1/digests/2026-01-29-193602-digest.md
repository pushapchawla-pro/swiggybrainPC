### Signal Fusion Formula
```
emerging_score = (0.4 * normalized_mom_search_growth) +
                 (0.25 * normalized_volume) +
                 (0.15 * google_interest_normalized) +
                 (0.1 * (100 - availability_pct)/100) +
                 (0.1 * zero_result_rate)
```

### Selection Criteria
- Minimum monthly search volume: 3,000
- Filtered to Personal Care BU (8 L1 categories)
- Ranked by emerging_score
- Top 50 selected for digest
